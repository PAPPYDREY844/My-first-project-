BIO

Davido, born David Adedeji Adeleke on November 21, 1992, in Atlanta, Georgia, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and record producer. Raised in Lagos, Nigeria, he rose to fame with his 2011 hit single "Dami Duro" and has since become one of Africa's biggest music stars. Known for his Afrobeats style, blending traditional African sounds with global pop influences.
